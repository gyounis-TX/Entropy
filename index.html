Lumenus Features

Everything trainees, attendings, and program directors need to manage procedure education.

📋

Case Logging

Log procedures in under 30 seconds. Select procedures, access sites, devices used, complications, and operator role. Attach notes and media.

✅

Attestation Workflow

Attendings review and attest cases with one tap. Bulk attestation for efficiency. Optional structured evaluations and feedback.

🖼️

Image Repository

Upload case images and videos. Build your personal portfolio or contribute to your program's shared teaching library. Auto PHI detection protects patient privacy.

⏱️

Duty Hours

Clock in and out of shifts. Track daily, weekly, and monthly hours. Export ACGME-compliant reports. Never miss a duty hour violation.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Filter by time range, PGY level, facility, attending, or procedure category. Bar charts, trend lines, and detailed breakdowns. Export to CSV.

🏆

Achievement Badges

Earn badges as you hit procedure milestones. Gamification that motivates trainees and celebrates progress throughout training.

CathCPT Features

Stop leaving money on the table. Get accurate CPT codes for every cath lab procedure.

🔍

Smart Search

Search by procedure name, code number, or category. Intelligent matching finds what you need even with partial terms.

🩺

Comprehensive Coverage

All interventional and diagnostic cardiology CPT codes. PCI, peripheral, structural, EP, diagnostic caths, and more.

📝

Code Details

See full descriptions, bundling rules, and common modifiers. Understand exactly what each code covers.

⭐

Favorites

Save your most-used codes for instant access. Personalize the app for your practice patterns.

📴

Offline Access

All codes available offline. No internet required in the cath lab. Works anywhere, anytime.

🔄

Annual Updates

CPT codes change every year. We keep CathCPT current so you never submit an outdated code.
